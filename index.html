What is ConDex?

ConDex is a retro-inspired contact manager for iPhone that turns your address book into a collectible adventure. Every contact gets a unique ConDex number, a custom pixel-art avatar, and a collectible card. Discover rare "shiny" contacts, never miss a birthday, and keep everything in sync with iCloud.

Built with SwiftUI for iOS, designed with love for retro gaming culture.

Features

ConDex Numbers

Every contact gets a unique number and collectible card.

Pixel Avatars

Create custom 16-bit character avatars for everyone you know.

Shiny Contacts

Discover rare sparkle-effect cards, hidden across your roster.

Birthday Reminders

Local notifications so you never miss an important day.

iCloud Sync

Your collection follows you across all your devices, privately.

Family & Groups

Auto-grouping for relations, plus custom groups you can reorder.

Privacy First

ConDex collects nothing. No analytics, no tracking, no developer servers. Your data stays on your device and in your own private iCloud account. Read the full Privacy Policy for details.
